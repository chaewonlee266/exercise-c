Important Figures and Organizations in the History of HTML

source: A history of HTML

Figures

Tim Berners-Lee
Inventor of HTML and World Wide Web--WWW
Introduced hypertext, URLs, and the idea of sharing documents on the internet
Dave Raggett
Expand the capability of HTML beyond documents
Proposed HTML+, later became base for research of HTML 3 which never became official
Began his own brower: Arena -- later developed by CERN

Organizations

CERN
European Organization for Nuclear Research
Where Tim Berners-Lee worked while developing the WWW and HTML
HTML and WWW were made to share research document among scientists
Organized the first World Wide Web conference
W3C
World Wide Web Consortium
Lead standardazing HTML and shaped modern HTML
Resolved conflicts between browsers throught standardazing
Led the development of HTML 3.2 and HTML 4
